Kigali Golf Resort & Villas

A peaceful place for relaxation, sports, and nature in Kigali

Kigali Golf Resort & Villas is a modern recreational and residential area located in Nyarutarama. It is known for its well-maintained golf course, fresh air, and beautiful views of Kigali city.

Things You Can Do

  • Play golf on a professional course
  • Enjoy natural green views
  • Relax and unwind
  • Take photos and explore the area
